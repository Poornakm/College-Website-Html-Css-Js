About Department

Vision:

To be recognized in the field of Aerospace Engineering, for imparting quality education and conducting fundamental research to produce competent engineers.

Mission:

  • M1: Impart Aerospace technical education through effective fundamentals.
  • M2: Create research ambience by providing state-of-the art laboratories, infrastructure facilities and skilled manpower for research and consultancy to face the current technological challenges in the aerospace field for the benefit of society.
  • M3: Strengthen industry-institute interface for developing team work, for developing sustainable, interdisciplinary research, internship activities and entrepreneur skills to develop young entrepreneurs with engineering ethics and social responsibility.
