Department of Food Technology

About Department

This group focuses on striving towards the safety and quality of foods through excellence in analysis by meeting international standards. Several fortified food products which have high market potential at an affordable price are being developed extensively by the team members. Food Technology lab has diverse equipment and machinery for pilot production of food products. Some of them have been developed in-house as well. Food technology team is also focusing on developing various scientific approaches to evaluate the physical preservation and several other processing methods for various food products.
