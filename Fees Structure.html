Jyothy Institute of Technology (JIT) is a Private Institute located in Bangalore, Karnataka and was established in the year 2011 under the leadership of Karnataka Rajyothsava Awardee Dharmika Pravara and Dr B.N.V Subrahmanya, visionary par excellence. JIT is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University Belgaum and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The Jyothy Institute of Technology Bangalore fee structure for some of the courses are as follows:

  • The Jyothy Institute of Technology fee structure for BE is INR 2,35,000 per annum.
  • Jyothy Institute of Technology fee for M.Tech is INR 1,28,000 per annum.
  • JIT Bangalore fee structure for BE {Lateral} is INR 59,400 per annum.
